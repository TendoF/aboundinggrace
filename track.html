How It Works

Real-Time Satellite Monitoring

From the moment your cargo is loaded, our tracking system monitors every move.

01

Booking Confirmed

Once your shipment is booked, you receive a unique reference number to track your cargo throughout the journey.

02

Cargo Loaded

Your goods are loaded and sealed. Our satellite system activates and begins transmitting the truck's real-time location data.

03

Live Tracking Active

Track your shipment via this portal or receive daily status reports by email or phone from our operations team.

04

Delivered & Confirmed

Upon delivery, you receive confirmation with proof of delivery. Final documentation is provided for your records.

Our Technology

Cargo Satellite Tracking System

Every truck in our fleet is equipped with a professional Cargo Satellite Tracking System that provides continuous real-time data on vehicle location, speed, and route status.

Real-Time GPS Location

Daily Status Reports

24/7 Operations Monitoring

Safety First

Built for Safety at Every Turn

Satellite GPS Tracking

All trucks monitored in real-time via satellite. Live location data transmitted continuously and shared with clients via daily reports.

Goods-in-Transit Insurance

All cargo is covered by our Zimnat Lion Goods-in-Transit insurance policy from point of loading to final delivery.

Experienced Drivers

All our drivers are experienced, licenced professionals with in-depth knowledge of Southern African cross-border routes and regulations.

Pre-Trip Inspections

Every vehicle is inspected before departure. Safety checks include brakes, tyres, lights, load securing, and documentation verification.

Scheduled Maintenance

Regular service intervals maintained for all trucks ensuring maximum mechanical reliability on every long-haul journey.

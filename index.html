FAQs

Common Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team.

What is your main cargo speciality?

We mainly carry coal from Hwange to anywhere around the SADC region. We also do other loads. Our current primary contractor is Khayah Cement Pvt Ltd.

What countries do you transport to?

We operate across Zimbabwe, South Africa, Zambia, Mozambique, Botswana, and the DRC. Our support service centers are located in Harare, Beitbridge, Musina, and Gateway.

Do you offer real-time cargo tracking?

Yes. All our trucks are fitted with a Cargo Satellite Tracking System. Clients receive daily reports advising the status of their load at any given time.

How do I get a quote?

All quotes are free. Simply call +263 772 816 090 or +263 712 578 654, email jimikchin@gmail.com, or fill in our online quote form. We respond within hours.

Is my cargo insured during transit?

Yes. We hold a Goods-in-Transit insurance policy with Zimnat Lion Insurance, providing cover for all cargo we transport across all our routes.

Common Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team.

What countries do you transport to?

We operate across Zimbabwe, South Africa, Zambia, Mozambique, Botswana, and the DRC. Our support service centers are located in Harare, Beitbridge, Musina, and Gateway.

Do you offer real-time cargo tracking?

Yes. All our trucks are fitted with a Cargo Satellite Tracking System. Clients receive daily reports advising the status of their load at any given time.

What types of cargo do you transport?

We handle bulk consignments, mining ore, agricultural produce, FMCG goods, and general consolidated loads. We specialise in overland freight across the Southern African region.

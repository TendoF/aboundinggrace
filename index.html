Who We Are

Abounding Grace
Logistics (Pvt) Ltd

We are a Zimbabwean-owned transport company committed to delivering your cargo safely and on time. With a fleet of heavy-duty international trucks and a satellite tracking system, we handle bulk freight across Southern and Central Africa. We mainly carry coal from Hwange to destinations throughout the SADC region, and also handle all other load types.

FAQs

Common Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team.

What is your main cargo speciality?

We mainly carry coal from Hwange to anywhere around the SADC region. We also do other loads. Our current primary contractor is Khayah Cement Pvt Ltd.

What countries do you transport to?

We operate across Zimbabwe, South Africa, Zambia, Mozambique, Botswana, and the DRC. Support centers in Harare, Beitbridge, Musina, and Gateway.

Do you offer real-time cargo tracking?

Yes. All trucks are fitted with a Cargo Satellite Tracking System. Clients receive daily reports advising the status of their load at any given time.

How do I get a quote?

All quotes are free. Call +263 772 816 090 or +263 712 578 654, email jimikchin@gmail.com, or fill in our online form. We respond within hours.

Is my cargo insured during transit?

Yes. We hold a Goods-in-Transit insurance policy with Zimnat Lion Insurance, covering all cargo across all our routes.

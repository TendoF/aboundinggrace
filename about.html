Our Story

Built From the Ground Up

Abounding Grace Logistics (Pvt) Ltd was incorporated on 5 November 2021 and began operations on 1 May 2022 with just 3 trucks. From day one, we set out to be different — a transport company that puts clients first, invests in technology, and never compromises on integrity.

Today we operate across six countries, serving some of the most demanding clients in the mining, agriculture, FMCG, and NGO sectors. Our satellite-tracked fleet handles everything from single consignments to large-scale contract haulage.

Our vision is to grow to 10 heavy-duty trucks by December 2030 and be recognised as the leading bulk goods logistics provider in the Southern African region.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

From 3 trucks to a growing regional fleet — here's how we've grown.

OCTOBER 2021

Company Incorporated

Abounding Grace Logistics (Pvt) Ltd officially registered under the Companies and Other Business Entities Act, Zimbabwe. Company No. 19347/2021.

MAY 2022

Operations Begin

Launched with 3 International Heavy Duty trucks and began serving clients across Zimbabwe and cross-border routes into South Africa.

APRIL 2022

Operator Licence Granted

Received Operator Licence Certificate (OP00002348) from the Republic of Zimbabwe — licensed for Goods transport operations.

2023 – 2024

Makomo Resources Contract

Secured major contracts with Khayah Cement Pvt Ltd (Sydney Mukundu & Judith Chinomona) and Makomo Resources Mine — primarily hauling coal from Hwange across the SADCC region. 30-ton loads, ongoing.

JANUARY 2025

PRAZ Registration

Registered with the Procurement Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe for Bulk and Cargo Transport Services (Category SB005).

MARCH 2025

Goods-in-Transit Insurance

Full Goods-in-Transit insurance policy secured with Zimnat Lion Insurance, covering all cargo on all routes.
